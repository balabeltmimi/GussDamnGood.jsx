What is Guss Damn Good?

Guss Damn Good is crafted ice-cream brand from 2 Thai students go abroad and stydy at Boston, USA.
They start to make the ice-creams which're from their passion. First flavour of Guss Damn Good is "Don't Give up #18"
Don't Give Up #18 is the name of eighteenth batch of fresh milk ice-cream - maybe it's the based ice-cream for the other flavours.
Without give up for making ice-cream, they can make the taste as suppose to be and be the one of the main flavour at Guss Damn Good.

How special in Guss Damn Good ice-cream?

With the passion in every flavour by crafters, this would be the one of speciality for the brand. Moreover, when you've tasted it, there has 3 steps to sense with ice-cream.

  1. One, Before taste, the first impression sense in the ice-cream will make you feel the identity of them.

  3. Three, After taste, gentleness would be the last, making you wanna eat and end of the scheme.

My Journey with Guss Damn Good

  • 1959 Without Any Time Machine

    • This journey was in the end of December 2019. I planned to visit my senior's rooftop with my friend at Udomsuk, Bangkok. I thought I'd much more time
    to go the other place that I want before the planned time. So, I went to Thonglor for grabbing some snacks (Butter Croisant from Paul) and try
    the new experience flavour, I'd never eaten ice-cream with having alcohol ingredient. The name of this flavour is "1959", the collaboration between
    Guss Damn Good and MINI, the flavour taste is dark beer with chocolate. When I tasted it, I felt that the dark beer's sense had come out first but there had
    chocolate which can be blended with dark beer. It's not bad at all. Unfortunately, at that time, I didn't think about collecting
    ice-cream lid. So, I'd thrown this lid into the trash. Until now that I'm collecting the lid, there has no revised batch for this flavour.
    It makes me feel sad that I threw the lid and I can't find the new one. Needing time machine and told myself in the past that

    "Not throwing the lid, please!

  • The revival of Cookie Bar

    • You would say that "Cookie Bar" from Guss Damn Good stores aren't having only some branches. I'd waited to go to Cookie bar via Guss Damn Good.
    But no slot of time to go, then the flavour from Guss Damn Good cookie bar has come out. Cookie Sauce Froyo, from cookie bar, is the flavour
    including cookie sauce, yogurt ice-cream and cookie ingredients. With the new things come out, I would like to taste as usual.
    Love at the first sight has been come out like the time I tasted Maine Rocky Coast. With the love, then I had bought this flavour more than others
    in the period of time. The revival of cookie bar turned to "Cookie Sauce Froyo" can make me love and reachable in the flavour.

  • First Meeting with co-Founder

    • In regard to COVID-19 pandemic, the relationship between me and co-Founder has created by responsing in Instagram story.
    Before pandemic, I'd gone to CTW and spectated the event "Scooper Competition". And then meet the one of co-Founder of Guss Damn Good in this event too.
    She's very kind to me and other customers and I want to discuss with her more. Since work from home, we had conversation about ice-cream via Instagram.
    I'd discuss about the flavour with two co-Founder and I'd told that their passion and the flavour of ice-cream can make me want to live again.
    I don't want to die because I would like to taste and walk in this journey. If I died, I'll no chance to taste it again.
    They're very kind to me and excited that I love their crafted ice-cream and we had made an appointment for meeting.
    We had a meeting after COVID-19 is subsided at Roast store in EmQuartier. We'd a lot of conversation and I'd seen their passion and
    their thinking of marketing in pandemic period. And also they told about the sense of the Guss Damn Good ice-cream.

    After our discussion, they gave me the special gift as above picture. This made me very pleased and thankful. And this's the first time for
    Guss Damn Good Cookie Bar Sundae. Thanks for the good conversation and this special gift. I'm very pleased to discuss with you.
